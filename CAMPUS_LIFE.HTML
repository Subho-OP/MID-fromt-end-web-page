Hostel

MID provides excellent in-campus separate hostel facilities for boys and girls. Designed aesthetically, all hostels are built to ensure a comfortable stay with plenty of air and light. The pastoral surroundings give the campus, especially the hostels, an ideal ambience to have a life of health, relaxation and most importantly education. Some of the key features are like:

  • There are four Hostels for boys and one Hostel exclusively for girls.
  • The rooms are equipped with wireless broadband internet facilities.
  • o ensure fitness and entertainment of the boarders, the hostels are equipped with indoor sports facilities like table tennis, carom, and outdoor games like volley balls, and badminton.
  • Some recreation facilities like television sets and cable connection are available in the hostel common rooms.
  • To bring an effective communication, STD/ISD and FAX facilities are also available.
  • Well equipped separate Gymnasiums for Boys and Girls.
