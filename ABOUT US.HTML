ABOUT US

Management Institute of Durgapur (MID) is a premier non-Government Management Institute in West Bengal with industry oriented research activities offering an unrivalled ring of education and research opportunities of outstanding breath and quality. It is located strategically on G. T. Road (NH-2) between Durgapur and Panagarh. This premiere Institute is the flagship Institute of Rahul Foundation. It offers various professional courses in Business Administration, Computer Application and Para-Medical Science approved MAKAUT. It has already established itself as a fastest growing Education Institute in West Bengal, with an established vision of preparing students in order to accept challenges of liberalized economy. Institute is affiliated to the Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology, West Bengal (MAKAUT, WB). Under successful manifestation of the dynamic vision of an enlightened industrialist, Mr. R. N. Majumder, MID is conceptualized on the reckoning of the growing need for high quality education in Eastern region. The Institute has already registered a hopping growth fulfilling the expectations of the students and the needs of the Industry & other sectors in the process of socio-economic development of the nation.
